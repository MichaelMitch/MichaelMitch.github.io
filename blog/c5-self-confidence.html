What is the relationship between mindfulness and self awareness?

Mindfulness is the the ability to pay attention to what's going around you, both internally and externally. Self Awareness is the ability to apply mindfulness to yourself in these regards, in order to control your emotions or to self reflect on what you are doing. They are very similar, in that they both deal with introspective awareness, though self awareness deals more on the self, and emotions, being able to pick up on changes in your body in different circumstances.
